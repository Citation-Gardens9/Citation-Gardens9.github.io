🧾 Dispatch for May 7, 2026 — Electrical Work Progress
Electrical work at the pool area continued today. Mr. Electric completed the panel connections at the far end of M‑building and finished the panel work inside the pool house. Power to the pump is now stable, but the pool house lighting remains off due to an issue with the old wiring.

A temporary solar floodlight has been installed to illuminate the pool area. Please use caution when walking on the sidewalks around the pool

🧾 Dispatch for May 5, 2026 — Mr. Electric (power interuption)
Katie at GNC spoke with Mr. Electric about possible power interuptions this Wednesday May 6, 2026. They let her know that power to the pool and lights attached to the breaker panels would be interruped, but wasn't sure about individual units.

🧾 Dispatch for May 3, 2026 — April Financials
We still don't have our April financials. We usually get them a couple weeks into the month. When we finally get bank viewing access, we will have up to the minute financials posted every month.

🧾 Dispatch for May 1, 2026 — Mr. Electric
Mr. Electric will be here on Wednesday, May 6, 2026 at 8:00 AM to complete electrical work at the co‑op. This includes maintenance on the breaker box at the far end of M‑building, installing a new breaker box in the pool house and sinking a grounding pole at both locations.

We are not yet sure whether electricity will be interrupted during this work, so please be prepared just in case.
