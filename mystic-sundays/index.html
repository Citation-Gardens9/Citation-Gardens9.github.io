Mystic Sundays With Trent

About the Series

A short weekly oracle or tarot message to inspire and guide you into the days ahead.
Each episode will be around 5 minutes long — perfect for a quick moment of reflection and magic on your Sunday.

What to Expect

  • One-Card Draw:
    A single card pulled with intuition and insight for the collective energy of the week.
  • Deck Spotlights:
    Occasional flip-throughs of new arrivals or favorite decks from the collection.
  • Gentle Guidance:
    Simple, uplifting messages — no heavy readings, just a touch of everyday magic.
